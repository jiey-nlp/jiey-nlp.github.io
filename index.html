About

Dr Jie (Jack) Yang is a lecturer (assistant professor) from School of Computing and Information Technology at University of Wollongong. He is working on natural language processing, a technique to enable computers understanding and using human language effectively. He focus on theory development, novel techniques and effective solutions of NLP in broad domains, such as Education, Law, Healthcare and Finance management. Specifically, his research covers the practical framework for question answering, knowledge graph, representation & reasoning, document retrieval, Chatbot (dialogue systems).

  • Interest: Machine Reading Comprehension, Information Retrieval, Model Robestness and Interpretation

Visual Question Answering

An attention-based classification model that aims at generating an answer for a given input image.

Accomplishments
  • Incorporated Convolution Neural Networks (CNN) for extracting image features and Long Short Term Memory for extracting question embeddings.
  • Tested the model on the COCO dataset, abstract scenes images, and got 69% overall accuracy on the VQA evaluation metric.
