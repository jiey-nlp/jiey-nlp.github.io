Jie Yang

Lecturer (Assistant Professor)
University of Wollongong
jiey (at) uow.edu.au


I am a lecture (assistant professor) in the School of Computing and Information Technology at the University of Wollongong, Australia.

My research lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Multimodality - with a special focus on developing robust and knowledge-enhanced systems for text understanding and retrieval. My research interests include adversarial robustness, document retrieval, knowledge graph reasoning, multimodal learning, and applied NLP for real-world applications.
